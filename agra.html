Agra - City of the Taj Mahal

Agra is synonymous with the Taj Mahal, one of the Seven Wonders of the World. A city with a rich Mughal history, it is home to magnificent forts, tombs, and gardens. Agra's architecture and cuisine reflect its glorious past, making it a must-visit for history enthusiasts.
