Jaipur

Jaipur, known as the Pink City, is full of rich history and beautiful architecture. You can explore forts, palaces, special observatories and more.

Here are a few must-visit places in Jaipur:

  • Amber Fort / Amer Fort
  • Hawa Mahal (Palace of Winds)
  • City Palace & Museum
  • Jantar Mantar (Astronomical Observatory)
  • Jal Mahal (Water Palace)
  • Nahargarh Fort

Activities to do in Jaipur:

  • Hot Air Balloon Ride 🎈
  • Taking an Elephant Safari 🐘
  • Shopping at Local Bazaars 🛍️
  • Enjoying Traditional Rajasthani Cuisine 🍽️
  • Attending the Light & Sound Show 🏰

Starting Price(₹):-5000

Duration:-3N / 4D

Leh-Ladakh

Leh-Ladakh, located in the northernmost region of India, is known for its breathtaking landscapes, high-altitude deserts, crystal-clear lakes, and ancient monasteries. It is a paradise for adventure seekers, nature lovers, and culture enthusiasts alike.

Popular Places to Visit:

  • Pangong Lake
  • Leh Palace
  • Magnetic Hill
  • Shanti Stupa
  • Hemis Monastery
  • Nubra Valley
  • Tso Moriri Lake

Adventure Activities in Leh-Ladakh:

  • River Rafting 🌊
  • Bike Expedition 🏍️
  • Trekking 🏔️
  • Camping 🏕️

Starting Price(₹):-12000

Duration:-5N / 6D

Varanasi

Varanasi, one of the oldest living cities in the world, is a spiritual hub of India. Known for its ghats, temples, and the holy river Ganga, it offers a unique glimpse into Indian culture and traditions.

Popular Places to Visit:

  • Kashi Vishwanath Temple
  • Dashashwamedh Ghat
  • Manikarnika Ghat
  • Sarnath
  • Assi Ghat
  • Ramnagar Fort

Spiritual Experiences in Varanasi:

  • Ganga Aarti 🙏
  • Boat Ride 🛶
  • Gali Exploration 🚶
  • Sarnath Visit 🧘

Starting Price(₹):-7000

Duration:-2N / 3D

Agra is home to the magnificent Taj Mahal and a city full of Mughal history. It attracts travelers with its royal forts, gardens, and architectural marvels.

  • Agra Fort
  • Fatehpur Sikri
  • Itmad-ud-Daula Tomb
  • Mehtab Bagh

Experiences in Agra:

  • Taj Mahal Sunrise 🌅
  • Mughal Heritage Walk 🕌
  • Shopping at Sadar Bazaar 🛍️
  • Petha Tasting 🍬

Goa

Goa, known for its sunny beaches, vibrant nightlife, and Portuguese heritage, is the perfect destination for relaxation and adventure. It is famous for its seafood, parties, and scenic landscapes.

Popular Places to Visit:

  • Baga Beach
  • Calangute Beach
  • Fort Aguada
  • Church of Our Lady of the Immaculate Conception
  • Dudhsagar Waterfalls
  • Palolem Beach

Fun Activities in Goa:

  • Water Sports 🏄
  • Night Market Visit 🌃
  • Dolphin Watching 🐬
  • Spice Plantation Tour 🌿

Travel Glossary

Itinerary
A planned schedule of your travel, including destinations, activities, and timings.
Transit
The process of passing through a place (like an airport or city) on the way to your final destination.
Backpacking
A style of travel where travelers carry all essentials in a backpack, usually budget-friendly and adventurous.
