Jaipur - The Pink City

Jaipur, the vibrant capital of Rajasthan, is known for its magnificent forts, royal palaces, and rich cultural heritage. A city steeped in history, it offers a captivating blend of ancient traditions and modern lifestyles. Explore the bustling bazaars, witness stunning architecture, and experience the timeless charm of this beautiful city.

Popular Places to Visit:

  • Amber Fort / Amer Fort
  • Hawa Mahal (Palace of Winds)
  • City Palace & Museum
  • Jantar Mantar (Astronomical Observatory)
  • Jal Mahal (Water Palace)
  • Nahargarh Fort
